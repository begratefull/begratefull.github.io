Comparing the Architectural Options

There are three main ways to build the bridge between SOLIDWORKS and JD Edwards. Each has significant implications for cost, time, and maintenance.

🛠

Custom P2P

A completely custom-coded solution. Offers ultimate flexibility but at a very high cost in time, money, and ongoing maintenance. Very high risk.

🧱

Dedicated Middleware

Purpose-built software (like CADTALK) designed to solve this exact problem. Intelligently transforms data with low risk and predictable costs. **(Recommended)**

⛈

iPaaS Platform

A cloud-based "integration hub" (like Jitterbit or Boomi). Powerful and scalable for many integrations, but can be overkill and costly for this single, deep connection.
